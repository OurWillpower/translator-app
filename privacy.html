Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Speakly (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our translation and speech features.

1. No Account Needed

Speakly does not require you to sign in, register, or provide any personal details to use our services.

2. What We Collect

We do not store your voice recordings, text, or translation history on our servers. All speech recognition occurs on your device using your browser’s built-in engine.

3. Third-Party Translation API

Your typed or spoken text is sent securely to the MyMemory Translation API to generate translations. We do not control their data retention policies.

4. Cookies

Speakly does not use cookies, trackers, analytics, or advertising identifiers.

5. Device Permissions

Your microphone is used only when you tap “Press to Speak”. You can revoke access anytime in your browser settings.

6. Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. You will find the latest version here.

7. Contact

For any concerns, write to us at: support@speakly.app
