Privacy Policy for Speakly

Last updated: November 20, 2025

1. Data Collection and Usage

Speakly ("the Service") is designed to operate as a secure, client-side application. **We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII), usage metrics, location data, or conversation history.**

Your input is processed anonymously via a third-party translation API (Google). This information is used only to return your translated text and is not retained by Speakly or WillPro Technologies.

2. Data Security

Since we do not collect any user data, there is no data to secure or store. All translations are handled securely over HTTPS.

3. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our operating procedures. We encourage users to review this page periodically.

**Contact Us:** For questions about this policy, please refer to our Terms of Service contact information.
