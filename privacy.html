Last Updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the Speakly mobile and web application (“Speakly”), developed and owned by WillPro™ Technologies, Systems and Services (“WillProTech”). Speakly is published and marketed under the consumer brand “The App Manufacturing Co.”.

1. No Account Required

Speakly does not require any login, registration, or personal information to use the free features.

2. What We Collect

Speakly does not store your voice recordings, typed text, or translations. All speech recognition is performed by your browser or device using built-in APIs.

3. Third-Party Translation Service

Speakly uses the secure MyMemory Translation API. Text you type or speak is transmitted solely for translation purposes. WillProTech does not store, log, or monitor this data.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Speakly does not use cookies, analytics tools, tracking pixels, or advertising identifiers.

5. Microphone Permission

Your microphone is accessed only when you tap “Press to Speak”. You may revoke permission anytime through your browser or device settings.

6. Data Security

Speakly handles no stored user data. All processing occurs on-device or is sent only for translation (text only).

7. Children’s Privacy

Speakly does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

We may update this Privacy Policy. The latest version will always be available at https://speakly.one.
